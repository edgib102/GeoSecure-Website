Service and Specialty Trades

Fleet management of specialty trades and services in HVAC, landscaping, plumbing, pest control and cleaning services, for example, often operate a number of different vehicles travelling to different locations throughout the day.

Maximize Efficiency

Use real-time GPS vehicle tracking to react to situations effectively and dispatch the nearest vehicle to respond to customers more quickly.

Analyse Routes

Plan routes and compare planned vs actual routes taken to review and improve operational efficiency.

Cut Costs

Review driving behaviour, speeding incidents, or be alerted to excessive idling to reduce unnecessary fuel usage and keep maintenance costs to a minimum.

Plan your drivers schedule

Use Geotab to optimise the route before jobs are dispatched. React to customer needs on the fly and allocate urgent tasks to the nearest driver. By expanding the solution with an in-vehicle display you can also include two-way messaging for driver communications, assign work and re-route drivers.
