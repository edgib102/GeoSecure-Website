Geotab Connect 2024 Overview

Geotab Connect 2024 is a premier event focused on innovation, sustainability, and the future of fleet management. This year’s summit will be held in Las Vegas, bringing together leaders in telematics, AI-driven fleet solutions, and key industry partnerships to shape the future of transportation and logistics.

Event Agenda

The event will feature keynote presentations from industry experts, hands-on workshops, and breakout sessions on topics such as:

  • Fleet Electrification and Sustainable Solutions
  • Advancements in Telematics and Data Integration
  • AI in Fleet Management: A New Era of Optimization
  • Networking with Global Partners

Sustainability at Geotab

At Geotab, we are committed to reducing CO2 emissions and promoting fleet electrification. Join us at the summit to learn more about our sustainability initiatives and tools like the EV Suitability Assessment and Green Fleet Dashboard, which help fleets operate more sustainably.

Explore Our Partner Ecosystem

Discover how Geotab’s ecosystem of partners offers tailored solutions for a wide range of fleet management challenges. From advanced telematics to AI and analytics, our partners empower businesses to thrive in a connected, data-driven world.
