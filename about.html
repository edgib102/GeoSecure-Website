About Us

Geosecure is dedicated to enhancing fleet management across various sectors with our tailored services and cutting-edge technology. Our solutions, including advanced GPS tracking, telematics, and dashcams, are designed to optimise operations, ensuring that your fleet remains efficient, safe, and productive, no matter the industry or challenge.

Empowering Your Fleet to Succeed

Geosecure provides advanced fleet management and in-vehicle camera solutions designed to enhance safety, efficiency, and compliance. As an authorised reseller of Geotab, a global leader in telematics, Geosecure offers businesses real-time vehicle tracking, driver monitoring, fuel management, and maintenance tools. Our vehicle camera solutions provide critical insights into on-road incidents, improving driver accountability and protecting against false claims. With tailored add-ons, Geosecure ensures your fleet operates securely, maximising operational efficiency and reducing risk.

Highly driven senior leader with over 20 years experience providing advanced software solutions that solve complex business problems.

Knowing that customer experience is at the core of every successful business, as director of Geosecure, Andrew Hutson is committed to providing advanced software solutions that solve the complex problems experienced in the transportation industry.

With a background in network communications, operations, product management and general management, Andrew's global experience spans three continents working alongside well-known brands such as Coles, Tesco, Fed-Ex, Samsung, Volvo & Wal-Mart.

Born in the UK and now residing on the Sunshine Coast with his young family, Andrew's clear vision is to identify demonstrable business cases for the specification of unique solution enhancement through client engagement.

Passionate about seeing his clients succeed, Andrew is results-focussed, knowledgeable and leads with openness, honesty and integrity.
